The miCube

The miCube is a simple, versatile, and modular fluorescence microscope that delivers high-grade microscopy measurements. We designed and built the microscope by combining off-the-shelf parts with a custom-designed aluminium body and open-sourced 3D printed plastic components. The miCube has different imaging modalities including regular super-resolution microscopy in both 2D and 3D, total internal reflection fluorescence (TIRF) microscopy, and epifluorescence microscopy. In contrast to most custom-built microscopes, the miCube can be built and aligned without being and expert in optics and engineering due to pre-aligned custom parts. For a detailed description of the complete microscope, go to the descriptive overview of the miCube.

miCube components

The miCube consists of roughly 30 components (see exploded view of the miCube below), which can all be 3D printed from plastic, CNC milled from aluminium, or commercially bought. Because no commercial components are used where these are not necessary, the price of the miCube is kept down. Go to the component list for a detailed view of all components, along with their availability and price.
